01

Brief

Kyndryl needed a credible design position for the AI era — not a slide deck of principles, but a working strategy tested on a real client engagement. The Bank of Thailand POC was the laboratory: a proof-of-concept for agentic AI in banking operations that would, if successful, become the foundation for a repeatable enterprise offering.

The brief was both strategic and tactical. What does AI-enabled UX actually mean when the "AI" is autonomous, goal-directed, and partially opaque? How do you design trust into a system that acts?

02

My role

UX strategy lead — I shaped the design framework for AI-enabled experiences and coordinated with Kyndryl's AI engineering and product teams.

  • →AI UX strategy — defining the design principles for agentic systems
  • →POC UX design and prototyping for the Bank of Thailand engagement
  • →Facilitation of AI literacy workshops for cross-functional teams
  • →Translating POC outcomes into a repeatable enterprise service offering
03

The challenge

AI-enabled UX has no established playbook. The most significant challenge was epistemological: how do you design experiences for a system whose outputs are probabilistic, whose reasoning is partially hidden, and whose actions can have irreversible consequences?

In a banking context, this is not theoretical. An agentic workflow that auto-resolves IT incidents or triggers escalation without human confirmation needs a UX layer that creates clarity — not anxiety — for the operators who oversee it.

04

What I drove

  • 01Developed the "AI-enabled UX" design framework — covering transparency, human oversight, and graceful degradation
  • 02Designed the interaction model for agentic workflows in the Bank of Thailand POC
  • 03Built prototypes that made AI decision-making legible to operations staff
  • 04Authored the enterprise service offering narrative — positioning Kyndryl as a trustworthy AI integration partner
  • 05Established Figma AI, Claude, and Maze as core tools in the Kyndryl UX practice
05

The outcome

  • →Bank of Thailand POC successfully validated the agentic workflow model in a live banking operations context
  • →The AI UX framework became the foundation for Kyndryl's enterprise AI service offering
  • →AI literacy and design tools embedded into the UX practice — measurable productivity gains for the design team
  • →Positioned Kyndryl UX as a strategic function in AI-era product decisions, not a downstream delivery team
