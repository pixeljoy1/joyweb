Mitra Jyothi — Accessibility & CSR

NGO · Social Good

Mitra Jyothi is a registered trust under the Indian Trust Act, located in HSR Layout, Bangalore — working for people with disability, and visually impaired individuals in particular, for over three decades. Founded in 1990 by Ms. Madhu Singhal, the trust's mission is to enable visually impaired individuals to lead independent and dignified lives through Education, Training, Counselling, and Communication & Technology.

The trust has served thousands of disabled people across Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Maharashtra, West Bengal, Bihar, Orissa and beyond. The design work supported Mitra Jyothi's outreach — shaping how an organisation that does profound, often invisible work presents itself to donors, institutions, and the communities it serves.

  • →Communication design for donor outreach and institutional awareness campaigns
  • →Print collateral — programme brochures, event materials, information systems
  • →Accessibility-first visual language — designed to work across literacy levels and contexts
  • →Digital presence design aligned with the trust's mission clarity

Illustration & Comics

Sequential · Visual Narrative

A parallel creative practice in illustration and sequential storytelling. Comics as a medium for ideas that resist reduction — agentic characters, machines as protagonists, and the quiet dynamics between people and technology.

  • 01Achhi Babu & The Machines — a noir graphic novel about a boy, legacy systems, and inherited purpose. Dark palette, precise ink linework, machine-age atmosphere.
  • 02El-Phazor — a colour-forward graphic serial with an ensemble cast. High contrast, expressive character design, kinetic composition.
  • 03A to Z PCOS — a health awareness illustrated guide. Accessible, non-clinical visual language for a condition affecting millions — designed for maximum reach.
  • 04Children's illustrated books — sticker-format characters, early literacy visual vocabulary, culturally specific illustration style.
