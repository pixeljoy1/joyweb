01 — APPROACH (INTRO)

Associate Director, UX Design — CTO Organisation, Kyndryl. I lead a focused UX squad dedicated to making structural UX improvements across complex, evolving enterprise ecosystems. From navigating the deep technical shifts of DevOps to shaping multi-cloud platforms, our work spans the full product development lifecycle and scales alongside enterprise maturity.

My practice sits at the intersection of traditional design foundations and AI-extended workflows. I do not merely translate strategy; I facilitate and evoke it. I believe in harnessing the speed and power of modern AI without abandoning the age-old methodologies — like visceral, pencil-and-paper systems thinking — that ensure long-term architectural integrity.

By balancing proven best practices with next-generation tooling, I guide teams to untangle systemic complexity and turn abstract business goals into tangible, high-fidelity UX that ships.
