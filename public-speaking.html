The brief

Speaking · CSR

Public speaking and representation of the Design organisation to the business community — engaging in public speaking and representing the Design organization to the business community, particularly the design@business community in Bangalore, was a rewarding yet challenging experience.

Effectively conveying intricate UX concepts to a non-technical audience demanded the ability to translate complex ideas into relatable terms. Learning to tailor presentations to cater to the unique interests and concerns of the business community taught me the art of bridging the gap between design and business, fostering a deeper appreciation for the symbiotic relationship between the two domains.
